GOAL SETTING

Goal setting is a powerful process of defining specific objectives and creating a plan to achieve them. It provides clarity, motivation, and direction in various aspects of life, including personal, professional, academic, and health-related goals.

Types of Goals:
-Long-term goals: These are major objectives you want to accomplish in the distant future, usually spanning several years.
- Short-term goals: These are smaller, achievable objectives that can be accomplished within a shorter time frame, usually weeks or months.
- Outcome goals: These are specific results or outcomes you want to achieve.
- Process goals: These focus on the actions and behaviors you need to engage in to achieve your desired outcomes.
