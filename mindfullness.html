MINDFULLNESS

Mindfulness is a practice that involves intentionally bringing one's attention to the present moment without judgment. It is often associated with various forms of meditation and has its roots in Buddhist traditions. Mindfulness has gained significant popularity in recent years due to its potential benefits for mental well-being, stress reduction, and overall quality of life.

Here are some key aspects and benefits of mindfulness:
1.Increased self-awareness
2.Stress reduction
3.Improved focus and concentration.
4.Emotional regulation.
5.Enhanced well-being
6.Better relationships
