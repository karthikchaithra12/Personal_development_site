MOTIVATION

Motivation is the driving force behind why we do what we do. It's what gives us the energy and direction to pursue our goals and desires. There are two main types of motivation: intrinsic and extrinsic. Intrinsic motivation comes from within and is driven by personal interest or enjoyment, while extrinsic motivation is driven by external rewards or consequences. Here are a few tips to help you stay motivated:
1. Set achievable goals: Break down your larger goals into smaller, more manageable ones. This will help you stay motivated and focused on your progress.
2. Find your "why": Identify your reasons for pursuing your goals. This will help you stay motivated during the tough times.
3. Celebrate small wins: Celebrate your achievements, no matter how small they may be. This will help you stay motivated and positive.
4. Stay positive: Focus on the positive aspects of your journey, rather than dwelling on the negative.
5. Surround yourself with positive people: Surround yourself with people who support and encourage you. This will help you stay motivated and focused on your goals.
